ASTR 1210 (O'Connell) PUZZLAH PREP QUESTIONS

PUZZLAH PREP: LECTURE 3


These puzzlah-prep questions relate to topics that might form the basis of in-class puzzlah questions for a given lecture. Consider them while reading over the Study Guide and reading assignment.

The topics are not guaranteed to be included as puzzlahs, nor would a puzzlah necessarily be formulated as are the questions here.


    If the universe is spatially infinite, as stated in Guide2, how many planets like the Earth is it likely to contain?

    If the stars seen overhead from a given location move in the sky from east to west during the night, in which direction does the Sun move during the day?

    True or false? Stars near Polaris, the ''north pole star'', never rise or set. Instead, they appear to move around Polaris in a counter-clockwise motion.

    True or false? "Constellations" are physical groups of stars that lie near one another in 3-dimensional space.
