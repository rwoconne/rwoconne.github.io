iCLICKER REGISTRATION



In order to receive credit for the in-class "Puzzlah" exercises, you must register your individual iClicker response unit.

Here's how to do that:

  1. Click on the following link to go to the iClicker Registration Website:

    http://www.iclicker.com/support/registeryourclicker//

  2. Enter your first and last name in the supplied boxes

  3. Answer "yes" to the question "Does your school use a Learning Management System?"

  4. Enter your UVA Email ID (e.g. htj47x) in the box labeled "Student ID"

  5. Enter the number on the back of your iClicker unit in the box labeled "Clicker ID"

After you have used the iClicker once in class to answer a question, your registration will be complete.

 Important! CONFIRM that your name is NOT on the following list:

  • UNREGISTERED students
