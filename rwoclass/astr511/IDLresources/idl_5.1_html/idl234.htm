The Macintosh Display Device

Device Keywords Accepted by the MAC Device

BYPASS_TRANSLATION , COPY , CURSOR_ORIGINAL , CURSOR_STANDARD , DECOMPOSED , FLOYD , FONT , GET_CURRENT_FONT , GET_FONTNAMES , GET_FONTNUM , GET_GRAPHICS_FUNCTION , GET_SCREEN_SIZE , GET_WINDOW_POSITIONrf , ORDERED , PSEUDO_COLOR , RETAIN , SET_CHARACTER_SIZE , SET_GRAPHICS_FUNCTION , THRESHOLD , TRANSLATION , TRUE_COLOR

The Macintosh version of IDL uses the "MAC" device by default. This device is similar to the X Windows device described starting on page The X Windows Device . The "MAC" device is only available in IDL for Macintosh .

To set plotting to the Macintosh device, use the command:

SET_PLOT, 'MAC'
