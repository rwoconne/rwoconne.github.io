Camera (focusing) Optics
... because the spectral resolution is a convolution of the grating function and the width of the image of the entrance slit.
(De)magnification of slit ~ fcamera/fcollimator
The camera/collimator system is an imaging system, which produces an image of the entrance slit on the detector. This usually determines the width of spectral features.
