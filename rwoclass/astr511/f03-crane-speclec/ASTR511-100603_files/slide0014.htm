Fan Mountain Observatory
Bench Spectrograph
Project Overview:
● Single object, fiber-fed, bench-mounted spectrograph
● Low to medium spectral resolution
● Primary wavelength coverage: 470 < l < 670 nm
      (although full visual coverage eventually)
● Target velocity resolution: 7-10 km/s
Instrument components:
● Focal plane module
● Fiber train
● Bench spectrograph
