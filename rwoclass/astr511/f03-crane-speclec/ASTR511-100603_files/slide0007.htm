Dispersing Element
• Can be a prism, etalon, grating, or grism
      (we will focus on the reflection grating)
• Reflection grating is basically a large number of long, but very very thin, parallel mirrors
• The grating as a whole acts as a mirror, but convoluted with the diffracting effect of all of the parallel reflecting lines.
• For a given grating line density, diffraction order, and angle a of collimated input, each wavelength has a unique diffraction angle b
ml = d(sina + sinb)
